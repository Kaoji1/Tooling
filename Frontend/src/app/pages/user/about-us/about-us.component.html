Objective

1.To provide employees with a convenient and efficient platform for submitting requests for materials, equipment, or services.

2.To streamline the request management process and reduce manual paperwork.

3.To enable tracking and monitoring of request statuses in real time.

4.To improve transparency and accountability in request handling across departments.

5.To create a centralized database of requests that can be used for reporting and future planning.
